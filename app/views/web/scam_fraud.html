Read carefully to fight Scams & Fraud

Who are scammers?

Follow these common-sense rules:

  • DEAL LOCALLY WITH FOLKS YOU CAN MEET IN PERSON - COE (Cash on Exchange) - follow this one simple rule, and you will avoid 99% of the scam attempts on Student 2 Student.
  • NEVER WIRE FUNDS, MONEY - anyone who asks you to do so is a potential scammer.
  • FAKE CASHIER CHECKS & MONEY ORDERS ARE COMMON, and BANKS WILL CASH THEM AND THEN HOLD YOU RESPONSIBLE when the fake is discovered weeks later.
  • Student 2 Student IS NOT INVOLVED IN ANY TRANSACTION, and does not handle payments, guarantee transactions, or offer "buyer protection" or "seller certification".
  • NEVER GIVE OUT FINANCIAL INFORMATION (bank account number, social security number, eBay/PayPal info, etc.)
  • AVOID DEALS INVOLVING SHIPPING OR UPFRONT PAYMENT and know that ONLY A SCAMMER WILL "GUARANTEE" YOUR TRANSACTION.
  • NEVER GIVE OUT FINANCIAL INFORMATION (bank account number, social security number, eBay/PayPal info, etc.). We don't collect it, so shouldn't any other user.
